Community Outreach

A key goal of this project is helping residents and visitors safely share space with moose in Summit County. Specific goals include:

  • Reduce negative moose-human encounters
  • Promote responsible recreation practices
  • Increase awareness of moose behavior
  • Share research findings with the public
