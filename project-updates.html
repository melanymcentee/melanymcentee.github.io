Project Updates

This page documents field activities, project milestones, and other updates regarding our study.

June 2026

Field season preparations are underway. Camera trap equipment has been purchased and site locations are being finalized. We're using a combination of iNaturalist, DWR moose-human conflict data, AllTrails data, and local news articles to find sites where moose-human habitat overlap has been documented. We are hopeful that we will have the study fully set up in early July!
